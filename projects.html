Pontaj

iOS • IoT • Arduino UNO • C • Swift • NFC • CoreLocation

The purpose of the project was to calculate the time spent a company's employees. They employees could use the iOS / Android app when they entered the company's building / offices and also when they left the premises. Also, each employee had an NFC token and they could use that instead of the app itself.

My job was to develop the apps (both iOS and Android) and I also built the device which read the token devices. Here are a few of the implemented functionalities:
  1. customer check-in / check-out using the GPS coordinates
  2. device's connection to Ethernet
  3. read / decrypt NFC tokens

Computy Gastro
